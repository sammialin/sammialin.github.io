Very nice cup of tea you will want to drink every day :)


    Ingredients

  • 1 Black tea bag
  • Boiling Water
  • 1 tsp Whole milk (can sub with cream, or non-dairy
  • 1 tsp sugar (or to taste)

    Directions!

  1. Boil the water and add to the tea bag
  2. Let steep for two minutes and do not do that thing where people dip the bag up and down you heathen
  3. Add milk and sugar to taste
  4. (Optional) Drink your tea with any small biscuits lying around. Enjoy a Jaffa cake. Or a scone!
